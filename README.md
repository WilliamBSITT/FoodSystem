# 🍽️ FoodSystem

> **Master your kitchen. Eliminate waste. Own your data.**

FoodSystem is an open-source kitchen inventory manager built for people who want full control over their food - and their data. Powered by Next.js and Supabase, it gives you a professional-grade workspace to track what you have, what's expiring, and what you need - without any black box.

---

## ✨ Features

- **Inventory tracking** - Know exactly what's in your kitchen at all times, organized and up to date.
- **Expiry alerts** - Get notified before food goes bad. No more forgotten leftovers or expired products hiding in the back of the fridge.
- **Shopping lists** - Generate and manage shopping lists directly from your inventory gaps.
- **Dashboard & statistics** - Visualize your consumption habits, waste trends, and stock levels at a glance.

---

## 🔐 Data Ownership

Your data lives in **your own Supabase instance**. FoodSystem never touches a centralized server - what's in your kitchen stays yours. You can export, migrate, or delete your data at any time directly from the Supabase dashboard.
